Small EdTech Firm

The Challenge: The team was spending over 15 hours per week on manual content creation and administrative tasks, leading to burnout and slowing down strategic initiatives. They felt they were "drowning in busywork."

The Result: Reclaimed 12+ hours of creative time per week and reduced curriculum time-to-market by 40%.

How We Did It

  • Clarify: Audited the full content lifecycle and identified the most repetitive, time-consuming steps.
  • Automate: Implemented a custom AI agent to automate the creation of first drafts for lesson plans and marketing materials.
  • Amplify: Trained the team on ethical AI editing to preserve their unique pedagogical voice while increasing output.
