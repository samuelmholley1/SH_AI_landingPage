A Method Designed for Humans, Not Machines.

The endless stream of new AI tools isn't the problem. The real challenge is the lack of a human-centered strategy for using them. Without a clear purpose, more technology just leads to more overwhelm.

That's why I created Reclaim by Design™, a proprietary method that goes beyond simple prompts and hacks. It's a holistic approach to building a lasting, ethical, and deeply personal AI strategy.

The work is not just about doing things faster. It’s about reclaiming the three most valuable assets you have:

1. Reclaim Your Time

Your most finite resource is being drained by repetitive tasks, administrative drag, and the mental energy it takes to manage it all. We will implement intelligent automation systems that eliminate these tasks, giving you back hours in your week—not for more work, but for more of what matters.

2. Reclaim Your Focus

Information overload creates strategic fog. It becomes impossible to distinguish the urgent from the important. We will work together to cut through the noise, clarify your highest-leverage priorities, and design an AI workflow that protects your focus and enhances your decision-making, turning complexity into clarity.

3. Reclaim Your Story

In the age of AI, the greatest risk is allowing your purpose and voice to be dictated by the algorithms of others. This is the deepest work we do. We use AI not just as a tool, but as a cognitive partner to help you amplify your unique insights, values, and narrative. We ensure that technology serves your story, not the other way around.

How We Do It: Our 3-Step Process

Phase I: CLARIFY

We start with a deep diagnostic of your current reality. We audit your assumptions, your workflows, and your goals. We get crystal clear on where the friction is and what "success" truly means for you, creating a strategic roadmap before we touch a single tool.

Phase II: AUTOMATE

Armed with clarity, we design and implement targeted AI-powered systems. This isn't about adding more apps; it's about eliminating tasks. We build intelligent workflows that handle the repetitive work, freeing up your time, energy, and mental bandwidth for high-impact thinking.

Phase III: AMPLIFY

This is where we move beyond efficiency and into mastery. Through personalized coaching, I help you learn to think with AI. You will move from being a passive user to a confident strategist, using AI as a cognitive partner to deepen your creativity, sharpen your insights, and scale your impact without scaling your stress.
