I Had to Rebuild My Own Life by Design.

An Expert in Systems, Running on Empty.

For over a decade, I was an expert at building systems for others. As a lead instructor and program director for elite test prep companies, and later as a director at a global EdTech firm, my job was to translate complex, intimidating systems into human-centered frameworks. I helped thousands of anxious, intelligent people manage their overwhelm, build their confidence, and achieve their goals. I managed multi-million dollar client portfolios and scaled high-touch programs for teams of over 60 tutors.

On the outside, I was a master of systems and productivity. On the inside, I was holding together a life built on a fragile foundation.

Highlights:

  • Managed $1M+ client portfolios.
  • Scaled programs for 60+ person teams.
  • Coached thousands of high-achieving professionals.

The Breakdown That Became the Breakthrough

For years, I was also a full-time caregiver for my wife, Ally, as she battled a cascade of severe, complex chronic illnesses. Her journey involved years on bed rest, multiple hospitalizations, and moments where we faced the very real possibility of losing her.

The pressure was immense. I was juggling a demanding career while managing a moment-to-moment health crisis, all while suppressing my own deep-seated trauma from a childhood defined by emotional neglect. I was running on a potent but unsustainable fuel: anxiety and a desperate need to hold everything together.

In the summer of 2022, that engine finally broke down. I hit a wall of profound burnout that left me unable to function.

The systems I had built for others had failed to save myself.

That breakdown forced a choice: continue to disintegrate, or find a new way to live.

From a Blank Page to a New Blueprint

The journey back was not about "powering through." It was about consciously and intentionally redesigning my life from the ground up, using the very principles I had taught for years.

I had to Clarify what truly mattered, separating external pressures from my own internal values.
I had to Automate the non-essential parts of my life to conserve my limited energy for what was most important: healing.
And slowly, I learned to Amplify my own voice and purpose again, using new tools—including AI—not as a crutch, but as a partner in my own cognitive and emotional rebuilding.

I learned that the most powerful systems aren't about technology; they are about humanity. They are about creating space for focus, protecting our energy, and aligning our actions with a deeper sense of purpose.

My Guiding Principles:

  • Clarify what truly matters.
  • Automate the non-essential.
  • Amplify your own purpose.

This personal journey became the blueprint for Reclaim by Design™.

Why I Do This Work

I am not an AI evangelist. I am a human advocate.

I believe that the age of AI presents us with the same choice my breakdown presented me: either we allow its complexity to overwhelm us, or we use it as a powerful catalyst to reclaim our time, focus, and purpose.

My work is dedicated to helping you choose the latter. I've been in the trenches of overwhelm, and I know the way out. It's not about becoming more like a machine; it's about using the machine to become more fully human.
